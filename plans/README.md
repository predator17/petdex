- **Route-cost sampling overhead in middleware**: rejected â€” measured ~55 ingest invocations/day at the 0.001 sample rate (documented in the 2026-06-04 ops report). Negligible; the observability is worth far more.
- **CSP header size (~1.7KB/response)**: rejected â€” real but trivial vs the levers above; not worth the review risk of touching CSP.
